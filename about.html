How it started

From a gym bag to a store.

It started with frustration. Our founder kept buying expensive protein products packed with ingredients nobody could pronounce. So they started making their own. Friends at the gym wanted them. Then strangers did too.

In 2022, Protein Haven opened its first location. The idea was simple — a place where every shake is made to order, every ingredient is traceable, and every customer knows exactly what they're drinking.

"We don't cut corners. If we wouldn't drink it ourselves, we won't make it for you."

Three years in, the menu has grown but the principle hasn't changed. Real ingredients, made fresh, for people who take their goals seriously. No shortcuts. No mystery powders.
